UNICON 19

Ever since the first UNICON held in Syracuse, New York in 1984, UNICON has been a festival for unicyclists, bringing riders together in every discipline from around the world. This event is not only a competition, but also a convention for the International Unicycling Federation. This year, UNICON returns to the Asia-Pacific region, Ansan, South Korea. We are excited and look forward to hosting you in our beautiful city. Our team has put together a comprehensive guide to help you navigate the event.
